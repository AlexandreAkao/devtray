How it works

Built for the way you actually code.

Menu-bar resident. Keyboard-first. Smart enough to read your clipboard before you ask.

Spotlight-style launcher

Press ⌃ ⌥ Space from any app. DevTray inspects your clipboard, guesses what you're holding (JWT? JSON? UUID?), and jumps straight to the right tool — pre-filled.

Snippets that actually scale

Save once, find from anywhere. Full-text search across title, body and tags. Pinned snippets surface in the launcher. Export to JSON, sync however you like.

100% local. Zero telemetry.

Tokens, snippets, inputs, everything stays on your Mac. No background uploads. No analytics. The only network call DevTray ever makes is the license check on launch.

Auto-update via Sparkle

Code-signed with an Apple Developer ID, notarized by Apple, stapled, Gatekeeper-clean. No "right-click → Open" theatre. Updates land quietly through Sparkle.

What we do NOT collect

Where data is stored

  • Cloudflare KV (Workers infrastructure, US/EU edge nodes)
  • Resend email logs (United States), 90-day retention
  • Paddle.com Market Limited (Merchant of Record) holds checkout and customer data per their privacy policy

Sharing with third parties

We share your email address with Paddle.com (Merchant of Record) at checkout and Resend (email delivery). We do not sell or share your data with advertisers or data brokers.

Your rights (GDPR, CCPA, LGPD)

You may request access to your data, deletion, or correction by emailing support@devtray.app. We respond within 30 days. For full account deletion, we will revoke your license keys, purge the KV record, and delete email logs we control.

Children

DevTray is not directed at users under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.
